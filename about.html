About Me

Background

I am currently a sophomore at Santa Clara High School. I grew up in California's Bay Area, in the midst of Silicon Valley, which is bustling with technology. I've taken multiple computer science classes inside and out of school, and I plan on taking AP computer science classes in the future. I'm also currently part of my school's STEM Leadership Institute which provides me many unique opportunities.

Interest in Computer Science

I always had an interest in computer science from a young age. Growing up, my dad was a software engineer so I was heavily influenced by his career, and by hearing from his experience, I decided engineering was something I also really wanted to pursue. When I first began coding, I was a bit doubtful. It was very intimidating for me. I didn't know how I could possibly grasp the concept properly, and I was very doubtful of myself. However, as I practiced, I improved a lot, and I really enjoy coding whenever I'm doing it. I now continue programming and improving my skills, participating in any opportunity I come across.

Hobbies

Whenever I have free time, there are plenty of things I enjoy doing a lot. I enjoy playing badminton for my school team and going swimming often. I do a lot of baking at home, and some of the things I enjoy doing most are traveling the world and doing photography.
